Udacity Android Developers Nanodegree

I earned the Android Developers Nanodegree in November of 2018. I first heard of the Grow with Google Challenge around Q4 in 2017, and decided to apply. After filling out the questionnaire, I awaited to hear my status. About 100,000 people had applied to this round of the challenge, and 10,000 were selected for the Android Developers Nanodegree. After an initial 3 month phase, the group was narrowed down to 1,000 to complete the full Nanodegree. It was a great experience, learning alongside a variety of different students across the globe. We had an active slack channel to ask for & provide help. I found android development a lot of fun, as it was my first real experience in what I consider a "full-stack" development environment. In addition, it was fun to learn how apps work, with concepts like recycler views, activities & fragments, various layouts, transitions, intents & more.
